Our Manufacturing Process

1

Design Analysis

We review your CAD files and specifications, identifying optimization opportunities for 3D printing. Our engineers assess material requirements, structural integrity, and production feasibility.

2

Material Selection

Based on application requirements, we recommend optimal materials from our extensive portfolio. Each material is evaluated for performance, cost-effectiveness, and manufacturing compatibility.

3

Prototype Development

We create functional prototypes to validate designs before full production. This iterative approach reduces risk and ensures final products meet all specifications and performance criteria.

4

Quality Control

Our Journey & Business History

2008 - Foundation
AlvexoHome was established with a vision to democratize advanced manufacturing through 3D printing technology. Our founders recognized the transformative potential of additive manufacturing and committed to making it accessible to businesses of all sizes.
2011 - First Expansion
Recognizing growing demand, we expanded our facility and invested in additional printing systems. This expansion enabled us to serve larger clients and take on more complex projects. Our team grew from 8 to 35 professionals.
2014 - Technology Integration
We implemented advanced software systems for design optimization and production planning. Integration of AI-powered quality monitoring enhanced our ability to maintain consistent standards across all manufacturing processes.
2017 - Industry Recognition
AlvexoHome received multiple industry awards for innovation and customer service excellence. Our reputation as a trusted manufacturing partner solidified, attracting clients from aerospace, medical, and automotive sectors.
2019 - Global Expansion
We established international partnerships and opened regional service centers. This expansion allowed us to serve global clients with reduced lead times and localized support. Our workforce expanded to 120+ professionals.
2021 - Sustainability Initiative
We launched comprehensive sustainability programs focusing on material recycling and waste reduction. Our commitment to environmental responsibility became central to our operational philosophy and corporate values.
2023 - Digital Transformation
Complete digital transformation of our operations introduced cloud-based project management, real-time tracking, and enhanced customer portals. These innovations improved transparency and communication with our client base.
2024 - Future Vision
Today, AlvexoHome stands as an industry leader with 2,847 completed projects and 156 active clients. We continue innovating, investing in emerging technologies, and expanding our capabilities to serve evolving market demands.

Materials & Capabilities

Our extensive material portfolio encompasses thermoplastics, thermosets, metals, and composite materials. Each material undergoes rigorous testing to ensure compatibility with our manufacturing processes and suitability for specific applications.

Thermoplastic materials including ABS, PLA, PETG, and nylon offer excellent mechanical properties and ease of post-processing. Thermoset resins provide superior chemical resistance and dimensional stability. Metal printing capabilities include aluminum, titanium, and stainless steel for demanding aerospace and medical applications.

Our composite materials combine multiple material systems to achieve superior performance characteristics. These advanced materials enable production of components with exceptional strength-to-weight ratios, ideal for aerospace and automotive applications.
